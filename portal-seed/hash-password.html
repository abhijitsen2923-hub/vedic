Hash a password for the Students sheet

Local utility — runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent over the network. Use this to convert a plaintext password into the bcrypt format the password_hash column needs.

— Hash will appear here —

How to use:

  1. Type the password you'd like the student to log in with.
  2. Click Hash it. The bcrypt hash appears below (starts with $2a$ or $2b$).
  3. Click Copy hash.
  4. Open the Students tab of the IVA Portal DB Google Sheet and paste into the password_hash column of a new row.
  5. Share the plaintext password with the student privately — don't save it anywhere yourself.

Why: The portal compares bcrypt hashes server-side, so the Sheet stores only the hash. bcrypt is one-way — even with the hash, the plaintext can't be recovered.
