Our Curriculum

A practical, structured approach developed to take you from foundational hand observation to accurate personal analysis.

What We Teach

Students learn hand shapes, elemental hand types, major and minor lines, mounts, markings, and interpretive principles used in palm analysis. The course helps learners understand how to observe details carefully and connect them to personality and life tendencies.

How We Teach

We teach palmistry through visual examples, guided interpretation, practical exercises, and case-based explanation so students can develop confidence in reading different hand patterns. The focus is on making the student capable of both accurate observation and meaningful interpretation.

Values & Opportunities

Why Learn Palmistry

Palmistry is a valuable subject because it offers immediate, personal, and visually engaging insight into human nature and life patterns. It can become a powerful spiritual consultation skill for learners who enjoy one-to-one interaction, observation-based analysis, and intuitive guidance.

Career Opportunities

Students can develop a profession as a palmist, spiritual consultant, personality reader, life advisor, or content creator in hand analysis and spiritual guidance. As with other occult sciences, professional opportunity grows when knowledge is paired with communication and practical application.
