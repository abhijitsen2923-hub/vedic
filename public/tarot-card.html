Our Curriculum

A practical, structured approach developed to take you from foundational card basics to intuitive, professional readings.

What We Teach

Students learn card meanings, Major and Minor Arcana, upright and reversed interpretations, spreads, intuitive reading methods, symbolism, energy understanding, and client reading structure. The course is designed to build both technical card knowledge and confidence in interpretation.

How We Teach

We teach tarot through practical reading exercises, guided demonstrations, interpretation training, and real scenario-based practice. This approach helps students move beyond memorizing meanings and learn how to conduct readings with flow, empathy, and professional clarity.

Values & Opportunities

Why Learn Tarot

Tarot is valuable because it blends intuition, symbolism, psychology, and spiritual insight into a tool that can help people gain perspective and direction. It is also one of the most accessible spiritual skills to learn and can be transformed into a modern profession through private consultations, workshops, digital sessions, and social media content.

Career Opportunities

With the right training, students can build careers as tarot readers, spiritual coaches, intuitive consultants, workshop hosts, and content creators. Online spiritual learning businesses increasingly position tarot as both a personal development tool and a self-employment skill.
